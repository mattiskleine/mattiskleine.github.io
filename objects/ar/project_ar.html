Plug 'n' Play Car Parts

View optional interior car parts in AR and switch them with the press of a button!

Concept

This project was done in collaboration with the Skoda Innovation Team located in the Czech Republic. Ethnographic studies showed the urge for and pride in DIY and customized products. This concept proposes:

  • Interior part design that allows easy exchangeability through electromagnetic mechanisms
  • Augmented reality enabled app that allows viewing of optional parts and purchase
  • Interior parts store with optional parts such as:
    • dashboard
    • head unit
    • steering wheel
    • speakers
    • glove compartment (freezer, microwave)
    • headrest
